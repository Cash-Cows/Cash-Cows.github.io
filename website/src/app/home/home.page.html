The Moo-Cow-Nomics

When someone sells their cow on OpenSea, a 10% fee is deducted from the sale called a “creators fee”. This fee will be sent to a smart contract, and allocated for every Cow holder to redeem evenly.

Community Milestones

Cow holders drive the roadmap of this project purely based on total sales volume.

FAQs

Immediately after mint out.

What is CC0?

CC0. This means there is no copyright. You can modify, distribute and perform the work, even for commercial purposes, all without asking permission so long as you own the wallet that owns the Cows until you sell it or transfer it to a wallet that you do not own.

What is the utility/roadmap?

100% of Creator fees are given to Cow holders evenly As rewards for passively staking. Roadmap is activated by reaching community milestones measured by total sales volume.

How do I stake and earn rewards?

Rewards are passively earned, meaning you only need to hold the Cows.

How can I redeem my rewards?

Current holders of Cash Cows can redeem their reward here or directly from the contract here.

If I sell my Cows can I still redeem?

Once you sell your Cow you no longer have the rights to redeem its rewards (it passes to the new owner).

How do I get on the Whitelist?

Please follow us on Twitter @wearecashcows to get info on getting on the whitelist.

What do I need to know before owning a Cow?

Rewards are driven by sales volume. No volume means no rewards. Before selling your Cow please make sure you redeem its rewards first. Failing to do so would mean the new owner would have the rights to its rewards instead.

How do you fund this project?

We fund this project through the rewards earned by holding Cows like everyone else. The community milestones measure the funding needed to activate milestones of the project.

Who is on the team?

Though this experiment is 100% controlled by contracts, the rewards are still financial in nature. We would like to remain anonymous until we work out the major issues that may arise while conducting this trial. Since we are all getting the same creator fees fairly and evenly, then we are all on the team technically.
