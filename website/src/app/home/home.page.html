WELCOME TO KOALATEE

Koalatee is a creative and artful collection inspired by endangered lovable marsupials. The project's vision is to build a unique quality of collections that embodies a purpose to generate and support revival and stability of habitat.

The Specs

Each Koala is unique and programmatically generated from over 342 possible traits, including expression, headwear, clothing, and more. All Koala are cute, but some are rarer than others and we have 15 1/1 legendary koala.

The koalas are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Polygon blockchain and hosted on IPFS.

The Perks

When you buy a Koala, you're not simply buying an avatar or a provably-rare piece of art. You are gaining membership access to a club whose benefits and offerings will increase over time. Your Koala can serve as your digital identity, and open digital doors for you.
