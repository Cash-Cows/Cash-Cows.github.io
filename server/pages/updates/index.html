Utilities 101

Cash Cows is a degen collection with over 14 utilities included. Here's what you need to know right now.

#1 How do rewards work?

When a cow is sold, a 10% creators fee is deducted from the sale. This fee is sent to a smart contract and allocated evenly to every Cow holder. You can redeem rewards on our website/contract.

#2 Be careful listing your cow!

If a cow is sold, the new owner will have the rights to its unclaimed rewards. If you're going to sell it, list it high so you won't be a sad cowboy later.

#3 Rarity isn't everything!

When buying a cow during mint, buy a cow that hasn't been purchased yet. ‚ÑπÔ∏è Untraded cows most likely have unclaimed rewards.

#4 ZERO listing gas fees.

Enjoy ZERO gas fees when listing cows on OpenSea, LooksRare and X2Y2. üëº Looks like someone is watching over you. üòá

#5 E-cow-nomics

Low Volume + Low Floor Price = Listed Cows Buying Cows = Volume = Cows Happy üòÄ
When in doubt, remember... "it's a free mint"

#6 Fair Play

The team earns by holding cows like everyone else. When we win, we all win. üéâ That's what we mean by sharing the wealth. üí∞

#7 Some cows need your help.

‚ÑπÔ∏è If you get one of these poor bovines, you can nurse it back to health and up its rarity along the way. ‚ú®

#8 Volume = Rewards

Rewards are sales volume-driven regardless of floor price. üìà High volume = High rewards. üö® Volume milestones unlock new experiences in the project.

#9 Burning Cows

When you burn a cow you will get steak and a milk bonus. You need 4 steaks to free mint into the new collection. When cows are burnt, the rewards for everyone else increases.

#10 What are Crews

There are 33 crews in Cash Cows. Some are popular, some are rare. üîé Find your crew & collect them all üß©

#11 Gallery

#13 Redeem Rewards

You can redeem rewards per cow. üö® Be careful though, redeeming rewards may not unlock your cow's full potential.
